# VIBE-100 Specification: Updated risks/mitigations shared with AI Steering Group

## Purpose
Establish a structured governance cadence for sharing updated risk and mitigation information with the AI Steering Group, with clearly defined milestone events throughout October, November, and December.

## Deliverables
1. **Governance Update Calendar** - Three milestone events with specific dates
2. **Risk and Mitigation Register (Updated)** - Quarterly updates for steering group
3. **Milestone Event Plans** - Agenda and materials for each update session
4. **Stakeholder Communication Schedule** - Regular updates between steering group meetings
5. **Decision Log** - Record of steering group guidance and decisions

## Scope
- Define three major governance milestone events (Oct/Nov/Dec)
- Establish cadence for risk/mitigation updates
- Prepare materials for steering group consumption
- Document decisions and guidance from steering group
- Ensure alignment and accountability on governance matters

## Milestone Events
1. **October Milestone** - Initial governance package and priorities
2. **November Milestone** - Implementation progress and emerging risks
3. **December Milestone** - Year-end review and 2025 governance planning

## Success Criteria
- Three milestone events scheduled with confirmed dates
- Governance calendar published and stakeholder awareness confirmed
- Risk register updated quarterly (at minimum)
- All steering group decisions documented and tracked
- No surprises - steering group fully informed of governance status
- Escalation paths clear for emerging risks
