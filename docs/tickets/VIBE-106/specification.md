# VIBE-106 Specification: Provide a list of relevant artefacts

## Purpose
Compile a comprehensive, organized registry of all governance, policy, and technical artefacts produced for CATH AI governance, and distribute them to appropriate stakeholder groups through their preferred channels.

## Deliverables
1. **Master Artefact Registry** - Complete catalogue of all governance deliverables
2. **Governance Team Sharepoint Folder** - Organized folder structure and index
3. **Confluence Documentation** - Wiki-style governance knowledge base
4. **AI Steering Group Distribution Package** - Curated materials for executive stakeholder
5. **Artefact Index and Navigation Guide** - Cross-referenced lookup for all materials

## Scope
- Document all CATH governance artefacts created to date
- Organize materials by category and stakeholder audience
- Prepare distribution packages for each stakeholder group
- Establish governance documentation library
- Create access and navigation instructions

## Artefact Categories
- Risk registers and mitigation plans
- Governance policies and standards
- Technical governance documentation
- Meeting minutes and decision logs
- Research and evidential materials
- Implementation progress reports
- Stakeholder communication materials

## Distribution Channels
- Sharepoint (team/organizational access)
- Confluence (enterprise wiki)
- Email (targeted stakeholder packages)
- Internal governance portal (if available)

## Success Criteria
- 100% of relevant artefacts catalogued and indexed
- Materials organized with clear folder structure
- Documented in both Sharepoint and Confluence
- AI Steering Group receives curated package
- Governance team can locate any artefact in <2 minutes
- Version control and audit trail clear for all documents
