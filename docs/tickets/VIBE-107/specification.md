# VIBE-107 Specification: General visibility of work in this SOW to TAB community and AI governance

## Purpose
Establish a structured communication plan to ensure CATH AI governance work is visible to the broader Technology Advisory Board (TAB) community and organizational AI governance structures, promoting awareness and alignment.

## Deliverables
1. **Communication Strategy** - Plan for ongoing visibility and engagement
2. **TAB Community Briefing Package** - Materials introducing CATH work to TAB
3. **Governance Channel Integration** - Connection points within organizational AI governance
4. **Regular Visibility Updates** - Cadence for ongoing community engagement
5. **Stakeholder Engagement Log** - Record of community outreach activities

## Scope
- Identify TAB community members and sub-communities interested in AI governance
- Develop tailored communication for different audiences
- Establish regular communication cadence (monthly/quarterly)
- Create awareness and generate feedback opportunities
- Link CATH work to broader organizational AI governance initiatives

## Key Audiences
- Technology Advisory Board members
- AI governance community of practice (if exists)
- HMCTS technology teams
- Organizational AI steering/governance structures
- Related projects and work streams (SOW030, etc.)

## Success Criteria
- TAB community aware of CATH governance work and progress
- At least 2 major briefing/engagement events held
- Visibility updates shared on regular cadence
- Feedback mechanism established with community
- Cross-team collaboration identified and initiated
- No knowledge silos about CATH governance approach
