# VIBE-97 Specification: Working with SOW030 to embed mitigations

## Purpose
Collaborate with SOW030 work streams to embed AI governance mitigations across the CATH service, ensuring alignment with cross-cutting initiatives and shared standards.

## Deliverables
1. **Mitigation Alignment Document** - Map CATH mitigations to SOW030 work streams
2. **First Draft Governance Package** - Initial submission to AI Steering Group
3. **Integration Points Identified** - Specific areas where CATH and SOW030 converge
4. **Stakeholder Feedback Log** - Record AI Steering Group input on draft

## Scope
- Identify overlaps between CATH and SOW030 mitigation approaches
- Document shared standards and practices
- Prepare initial governance materials for steering group review
- Establish feedback mechanism for group input

## Key Outcomes
- SOW030 alignment confirmed in writing
- AI Steering Group has reviewed initial package
- Foundations established for ongoing coordination
- Clear integration points documented for implementation teams

## Timeline
- Weeks 1-2: SOW030 work stream analysis and mapping
- Weeks 3-4: First draft preparation
- Week 5: AI Steering Group presentation

## Success Criteria
- All key mitigation areas mapped to SOW030 streams
- First draft submitted to steering group
- Feedback mechanism established
- No critical gaps identified between approaches
