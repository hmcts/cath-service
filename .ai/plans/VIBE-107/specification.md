# VIBE-107: General visibility of work in this SOW to TAB community and AI governance - Specification

## Problem Statement
General visibility of work in this SOW to TAB community and AI governance.

## User Story
**AS A** Program Lead
**I WANT** to provide regular visibility of VIBE project work to the TAB community and AI governance
**SO THAT** stakeholders are informed, engaged, and can provide input or support as needed

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Communication plan created for TAB community and AI governance
- [ ] Regular update cadence established
- [ ] Communication channels identified and configured
- [ ] Content format and templates created
- [ ] First communication/update delivered
- [ ] Ongoing updates delivered per schedule
- [ ] Feedback mechanism established
- [ ] Engagement metrics tracked

## Technical Context
TAB likely refers to "Technical Approval Board" or similar technical governance body. This ticket ensures the broader technical community and AI governance stakeholders have visibility into VIBE project activities, progress, challenges, and learnings.

## Target Audiences

### TAB Community
- Technical architects
- Technical leads
- Senior developers
- Platform teams
- Security team
- Other technical governance bodies

### AI Governance
- AI Steering Group (covered in VIBE-100 but may need supplementary comms)
- AI ethics committee
- Data governance team
- Compliance team
- Risk management

## Communication Objectives

### Awareness
- Project exists and what it's about
- Key milestones and timelines
- How to get involved or learn more

### Transparency
- Progress updates
- Challenges and how they're being addressed
- Decisions made and rationale
- Metrics and results

### Engagement
- Opportunities to provide input
- Requests for support or collaboration
- Invitations to demos or showcases
- Feedback channels

### Knowledge Sharing
- Lessons learned
- Best practices discovered
- Technical insights
- Reusable patterns or tools

## Communication Channels

### Regular Updates
- **Monthly Newsletter/Email**: Summary of progress, achievements, challenges
- **Quarterly Showcase**: Demos, deep dives, Q&A sessions
- **TAB Meeting Presentations**: As invited or scheduled
- **Intranet/Portal**: Dedicated project page with latest updates

### Passive Visibility
- **Confluence Space**: Project documentation accessible to TAB community
- **Slack/Teams Channel**: Open channel for questions and discussions
- **Metrics Dashboard**: Real-time view of progress and KPIs
- **Blog Posts**: Key learnings and insights

### On-Demand
- **Office Hours**: Regular times for anyone to ask questions
- **Project Contact**: Clear point of contact for inquiries
- **Demo Requests**: Ability to request focused demonstrations

## Content Topics

### Project Overview
- Objectives and scope
- Approach and methodology
- Team composition
- Timeline and milestones

### Progress Updates
- Milestones achieved
- Current focus areas
- Upcoming activities
- Percentage complete

### Technical Insights
- Architecture decisions
- Tool evaluations
- Integration approaches
- Technical challenges and solutions

### Results and Impact
- Metrics and KPIs
- Productivity improvements
- Quality outcomes
- Developer feedback

### Risks and Issues
- Current risks (appropriate detail level)
- How risks are being mitigated
- Issues requiring input or support
- Lessons learned

### Opportunities for Engagement
- Ways to get involved
- Feedback requested
- Collaboration opportunities
- Learning and training events

## Update Frequency

**Monthly**:
- Email update/newsletter
- Metrics dashboard refresh
- Portal page update

**Quarterly**:
- Showcase/demo session
- Detailed blog post
- TAB presentation (if scheduled)

**Ad-hoc**:
- Major milestone announcements
- Critical decisions requiring input
- Issue escalations
- Success stories

## Content Formats

### Executive Summary (1 page)
- Highlights
- Progress snapshot
- Key metrics
- Next steps

### Detailed Update (2-3 pages)
- Progress by workstream
- Technical deep dive on selected topic
- Metrics and trends
- Risks and issues
- Upcoming activities

### Visual Dashboard
- Progress indicators
- Key metrics
- Risk RAG status
- Timeline visualization

### Presentation Deck (10-15 slides)
- For TAB meetings or showcases
- Mix of overview and deep dive
- Interactive demos where appropriate

## Out of Scope
- Detailed technical implementation documentation (available but not pushed)
- Real-time status updates (monthly cadence sufficient)
- Individual stakeholder management (handled separately)

## Dependencies
- Access to communication channels (email lists, Confluence, Teams/Slack)
- Project metrics and status data
- Artefacts list (VIBE-106) for linking to detailed resources
- TAB meeting schedule for presentation slots
