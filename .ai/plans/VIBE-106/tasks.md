## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Artefacts scattered across platforms | High | Centralize in fewer locations |
| Owners decline responsibility | Medium | Escalate to management |
| Broken links after reorganization | High | Test thoroughly before rollout |
| Low stakeholder engagement | Medium | Follow up personally with key groups |
| Register becomes outdated | High | Schedule regular maintenance |
| Access permission issues | Medium | Document clear escalation process |
